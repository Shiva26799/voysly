Everything you need to know about VOYSLY's AI voice interviewing platform.

VOYSLY uses advanced conversational AI to conduct real-time voice interviews. When a candidate calls in or clicks an interview link, our AI greets them, asks role-specific questions, listens to their responses, and asks intelligent follow-up questions based on their answers — just like a skilled recruiter would. Each interview is scored, transcribed, and summarized automatically.
We're transparent with candidates that they're interviewing with an AI assistant. That said, our voice AI is incredibly natural — with conversational pacing, empathy cues, and contextual understanding. 94% of candidates rate their experience as positive, and many prefer the flexibility of interviewing on their own schedule.
